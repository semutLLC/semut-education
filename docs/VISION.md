# VISION

AI is rapidly reducing the cost of creating software.

Semut believes this change should make software creation more accessible rather than more exclusive.

Our vision is to help people learn by participating in real projects, collaborating with others, and continuously improving through customer feedback.

Education should not end after completing a course. It should continue through contribution, experimentation, and practical experience.

Semut Education connects learning directly with building.

Instead of separating education from real software development, we aim to integrate them into a continuous cycle of learning, contribution, feedback, and improvement.
