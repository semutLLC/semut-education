# BUSINESS MODEL

Semut Education is intended to be an open educational resource.

Core educational materials remain publicly available.

Potential long-term offerings include:

* Guided onboarding for contributors
* Team training for AI-assisted development
* Workshops for businesses adopting AI workflows
* Consulting on engineering processes
* Enterprise training programs
* Community mentorship
* Certification based on practical contributions rather than examinations

The primary objective is to lower the barrier to effective software development while strengthening the Semut open-source ecosystem.
